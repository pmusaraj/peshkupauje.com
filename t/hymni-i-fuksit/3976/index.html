Dridhen çafkat e rënkojnë, përmbi detin përpëliten; janë gati ato të struken

njëherësh…

Në tërbimin e gjëmimit lodhje sheh e ndjen demoni; ka besim zogu i furtunës;

His story, dated to 1932, is that of a 13-year-old boy who denounced his father to the authorities and was in turn killed by his family.
