Happy Hour (2015 film)

Happy Hour (ハッピーアワー, Happī Awā) is a 2015 Japanese film directed by Ryūsuke Hamaguchi. The film follows the lives and loves of four middle-class women in their thirties who are friends and who live in Kobe. When one reveals she is undergoing divorce proceedings, the others begin to rethink their relationships.
