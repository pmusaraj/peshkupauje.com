Fear of the dark

Fear of the dark is a common fear or phobia among children and, to a varying degree, adults. A fear of the dark does not always concern darkness itself; it can also be a fear of possible or imagined dangers concealed by darkness. Some degree of fear of the dark is natural, especially as a phase of child development. Most observers report that fear of the dark seldom appears before the age of 2 years. Dark Night of the Soul

It follows after the second phase, the illumination in which God's presence is felt, but this presence is not yet stable.
